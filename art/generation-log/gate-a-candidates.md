## Common Prompt Constraints

- Original game art only; no named artist/game imitation.
- Do not paste or trace the A-H reference sheets.
- No real brands, copyrighted marks, or tiny readable shop-text dependence.
- Restrained cyan/magenta accents; no global full-saturation cyberpunk.
- Player silhouette, collision edge, and menu hierarchy remain readable.
- No blood, gore, watermark, or full-stage completion claims.
